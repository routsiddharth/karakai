Karak is strong tea boiled down with evaporated milk, cardamom and a frankly irresponsible amount of sugar. It went from Gulf cafeterias to American strip malls without anyone keeping a list. So we keep the list.

Every pin is a real shop we looked up ourselves: Yemeni coffee houses pouring adeni, Pakistani dhabas doing doodh patti, karak counters. Hours, prices and ratings come from public listings. When we couldn't confirm a price we put a ~ in front of our best guess.
